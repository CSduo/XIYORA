Section 2: What Natural Latex Actually Means

Publish as a two-column section. Left: visual of rubber tree tapping. Right: body copy below.

Natural latex begins with a small wound — a scored incision in the bark of Hevea brasiliensis, the Para rubber tree. The tree responds by producing a milky sap. Collected in cups hung at the base of each cut, this sap is the raw material for the world's finest sleep surfaces.

Dunlop processing — the original method, invented in 1929 — froths the sap, pours it into a mould, and vulcanises it. The result is a dense, durable core that holds its shape for decades. Talalay processing adds a vacuum and flash-freezing step, creating a more open-cell, breathable, and lighter foam — the choice for pillows and comfort layers.

Both are entirely natural. Neither contains the petroleum-derived compounds found in polyurethane foam. Both are inherently anti-microbial, dust-mite resistant, and hypoallergenic. The material does not off-gas. It does not compress permanently. It simply works — for decades.

Section 3: The Bingxi Partnership

XIYORA × Bingxi — Official Global Distribution Partnership

Bingxi's manufacturing facilities operate to LGA certification standards, with OEKO-TEX compliance across their latex product range. As an official Bingxi partner, XIYORA is one of a small number of globally authorised distributors with verified, traceable access to Bingxi's production pipeline.

This means: consistent density tolerances, documented batch records, and products you can specify with confidence — whether you are outfitting a bedroom or a 150-room boutique hotel.

The Firm option (40 ILD) is favoured by back sleepers and those managing lumbar conditions. The Medium (28 ILD) suits side sleepers. The Plush (20 ILD) is recommended as a dedicated comfort layer.

08 — UI / UX & Technical Fixes

Priority 1 — Critical Fixes (Week 1)

FIX SPECIFICATION

Location / Delivery Zone Auto-detection Not Working

Product Filter: Missing ILD / Firmness

Add filter dimensions: Firmness (Plush / Medium / Firm), Material Type (Dunlop / Talalay), Thickness (100mm / 150mm / 200mm), Size, Price Range, Use Case (B2C / B2B). Current filter is purely categorical — not useful for a buyer comparing specifications.

WhatsApp Modal Not Prominent Enough

The B2C WhatsApp CTA should appear as a sticky bottom bar on mobile AND a floating button on desktop. The current modal trigger is buried. Distinct messaging for B2C ('Ask about a product') vs B2B ('Request trade pricing') funnels.

Priority 2 — Design & Visual Upgrades (Week 2–3)

Typography

Current: Adequate. Upgrade to: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body). This pairing communicates editorial luxury instantly. Apply consistent type scale: 48px H1, 32px H2, 24px H3, 16px body, 14px caption.

Colour System

Formalise the palette. Primary: Deep Navy #1B3A6B. Secondary: Warm Gold #B8860B. Neutral 1: Cream #FDF6EC. Neutral 2: Warm White #FAFAF8. Text: Espresso #1A1A1A. Muted: #5C5C5C. Never use pure black (#000) or pure white (#FFF) — both read cheap.

Product Photography Brief

Every product needs: (1) Pure white background hero shot. (2) Cross-section cut shot showing the latex core. (3) Lifestyle shot in a styled bedroom. (4) Detail shot of texture and cover material. (5) Scale shot with human hand for context. Use warm, natural light — no flash backgrounds.

Desktop Layout Improvement

Current desktop layout is single-column with too much white space. Implement a 12-column grid. Product pages: 60/40 split (image gallery left, specs + CTA right). Category pages: 3-column card grid. Hover states on all cards: slight shadow lift + gold border reveal.

Mobile Layout

Navigation: hamburger with full-screen overlay. Product images: swipe gallery. Specs table: horizontal scroll on mobile (not collapsed). Sticky add-to-cart / enquire CTA bar at bottom of every product page on mobile.

Loading States

All images: lazy load with blur placeholder. First Contentful Paint target: under 1.5s. Add skeleton loaders to product cards — prevents layout shift.

5-question guided tool: sleeping position → firmness type → use (solo/couple) → health considerations → budget. Output: specific product recommendation with explanation. Dramatically increases engagement and reduces decision paralysis.

Comparison Tool

Allow user to select up to 3 products and compare side-by-side on: material, ILD, thickness, price, guarantee, certifications. Priority for serious buyers.

Live Chat Widget
